Week 6

This was a busy week. We had to print 5 stickers, 3 of them being single-colored using the Roland GS-24 CAMM 1, and 2 of them being multi-colored using the Roland 540i printer. We also had to make a mold of an object that we create in Fusion360. We then casted that design in wax and then silicon so that we can make chocolate in the molds and still have it be edible. We also made a coaster using urethane and epoxy. We dyed the first solution a certain color, and I chose blue. Then we put our object in the after the solution dried. Then we poured a clear solution on top to allow a transparent view into the coaster. Mr. Gerber gave me two Chinese coins as my item and it looks pretty cool.
